Ganesh Chaturthi is a ten-day festival that typically falls in the Hindu month of Bhadra (August or September).
The celebrations commence with the installation of beautifully crafted clay idols of Lord Ganesha in homes and public places. These idols are worshipped with great devotion during the ten-day period.
